Scoring

The scoring works as follows:
  • Higher tiers of characters will be worth more points to guess correctly
  • "Dies by": the week selected is the same as or after the week the character dies
  • "Dies on": the week selected is the exact week the character dies
  • "Lives": at the end of the season, if the character is still alive you get these points

    • Notes about scoring:

  • "Dies" means the first episode we hear of the character's death
  • If a character comes back to life, their week of death will be the latest week they die, or none if they are still alive at the end
  • If a character becomes a white walker, they are still dead. The night king is the only "alive" white walker for our rules

    • Payout Structure

  • 1st Place: $140
  • 2nd Place: $30
  • 3rd Place: $10
